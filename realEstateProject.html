Demo

The following series of short videos illustrates the process of closing a deal. The scenario is that a customer comes to the office wanting to offer their house for sale. So, we add that customer to the application along with his offer. After that, we get some demand and enter the information related to the demand. Finally, we find a matching demand to the offer and close the deal after approving all relavent payments.
